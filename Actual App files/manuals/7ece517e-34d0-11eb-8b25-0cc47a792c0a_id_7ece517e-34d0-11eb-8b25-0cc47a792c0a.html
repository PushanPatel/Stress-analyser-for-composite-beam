The terminology used in coding
Symbol
Quantity it represents
N
Number of materials
b(i)
Width of each material starting from below (i = 1, 2, ... ,N)
E(i)
Modulus of elasticity of each material starting from below (i = 1,2,... ,N)
h(i)
Height of each material starting from below (i = 1, 2, ... ,N)
H
Total height of the cross section
n(i)
Ratio of modulus (n(i) = E(i)/E(1)) (i = 1, 2, ... ,N)
sum(i)
Sum of heights after each material (sum(i) = h(1) + h(2) + ... + h(i-1))
y(i)
Distance of centroid of each material from origin in transformed section
A(i)
Area of each material cross section in transformed section
sumA
Total area of transformed cross-section
sumAy
A(1)y(1) + A(2)y(2) + ... + A(N)y(N)
yc
Distance of centroid from origin
I(i)
Moment of inertia of each material in transformed cross section
Itr
Moment of inertia of transformed cross-section
M
Bending Moment
P
Axial Load
sigma1, sigma2
Normal stresses at extremes of the cross-section (one is maximum, one is
minimum)
